# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on performing a deep dive analysis of an e-commerce sales dataset and building an interactive dashboard to uncover key business insights. The objective is to analyze sales performance, identify patterns and trends, and present insights through an interactive dashboard.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales data to identify patterns and trends.
- Define key performance indicators (KPIs) for business performance.
- Perform deep dive analysis on sales and profit data.
- Build an interactive dashboard to visualize business insights.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains e-commerce sales data with the following fields:

- Order Date – Date when the order was placed
- Product Name – Name of the product
- Category – Product category
- Region – Sales region
- Quantity – Number of items sold
- Sales – Total sales amount
- Profit – Profit generated from the sale

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs were used to evaluate business performance:

- Total Sales – Total revenue generated from all sales
- Total Profit – Total profit earned from sales
- Total Orders – Total number of orders placed
- Average Order Value – Average revenue generated per order

## Deep Dive Analysis

The deep dive analysis focuses on identifying patterns and trends in the dataset using different visualizations.

The following analyses were performed:

- Sales by Region
- Profit by Category
- Top 5 Products by Sales
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales and Profit by Category

These analyses help understand which regions and product categories contribute the most to overall business performance.

## Interactive Dashboard

An interactive dashboard was created using Power BI to visualize key business insights. The dashboard includes:

- KPI cards showing important business metrics
- Sales distribution across regions
- Profit comparison by category
- Top performing products
- Monthly sales trend visualization
- Interactive filters for Region and Category

The dashboard allows users to explore the data dynamically and gain meaningful insights.

## Insights

1. The total sales are 11M and the total profit is 1.84M, which shows the business is generating good revenue.
2. The West region has the highest sales, while the North region has the lowest sales.
3. Electronics category generates the highest profit compared to Accessories and Office.
4. Among products, Camera has the highest sales, followed by Monitor and Printer.
5. The monthly sales trend shows the highest sales in May and the lowest in February.
6. Overall, the Electronics category performs best in both sales and profit.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization techniques can help businesses understand sales performance and make better decisions. The interactive dashboard provides a clear and intuitive view of key business metrics.
