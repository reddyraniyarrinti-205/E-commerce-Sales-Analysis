# Task-2 :Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence Analysis

## Objective
The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the dataset to identify patterns, trends, and relationships within the data. The analysis helps in understanding business performance and generating insights that support data-driven decision making. SQL queries are also used to answer key business questions, and a static dashboard is created to summarize important business metrics.

## 1. Descriptive Statistics & Univariate Analysis
In this stage, the dataset was explored to understand its structure and statistical characteristics. Summary statistics such as mean, minimum, maximum, and distribution of values were analyzed to gain a basic understanding of the data.

Univariate analysis was performed to study individual variables and their distributions using visualizations such as bar charts and histograms. This helped identify patterns in sales, profit, and product categories.

## 4. Static Dashboard Mock-up
Based on the insights obtained from the EDA process, a static dashboard was created to visualize key business performance indicators.

The dashboard provides a quick overview of the business performance using important KPIs and charts.

Key metrics displayed in the dashboard include:

Total Sales: ₹1,06,67,881  
Total Profit: ₹18,44,665  
Total Orders: 3500  
Average Order Value: ₹3047.97  

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

- Profit by Region
- Sales by Product Category
- Year-wise Sales Trend
- Category-wise Sales Distribution

## Key Insights

- West region generated the highest profit among all regions.
- North region recorded the lowest profit.
- Electronics category contributed the highest sales.
- Office category has the smallest share of sales.
- Sales vary across different months based on the year-wise sales trend.
- The business generated over ₹1.06 crore in sales with ₹18.4 lakh profit from 3500 orders.
